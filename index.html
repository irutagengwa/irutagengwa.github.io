Rwanda

Rwanda is a landlocked country situated in central Africa. Also known as 'The Land of a Thousand Hills', Rwanda has five volcanoes, 23 lakes and numerous rivers, some forming the source of the River Nile. Rwanda is also a place of mass tourism and if you're most likely a tourist, you will need to know this list of top 10 restaurants in Rwanda ranging all the way from Chinese to Japanese food.
